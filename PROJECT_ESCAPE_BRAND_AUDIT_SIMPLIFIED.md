## PAGE 1: WHAT IS PROJECT-ESCAPE?

Project-Escape is a technology company that solves a critical problem in artificial intelligence: **AI systems are too expensive to talk to each other.**

When one AI system sends a message to another AI system, that message costs money. At global scale, with billions of AI conversations happening every day, each conversation costs between 50 cents and $5 dollars in token fees. This is unsustainable.

**Project-Escape's Solution:**

Project-Escape has created a compression system called the **Adriana Protocol** that reduces the size of AI messages by 96%. Instead of sending 800 tokens (which costs $0.50-$5.00), you send 3 glyphs (which costs almost nothing).

This is the core value proposition: **Same message, 96% smaller, 96% cheaper.**

The system also adds security: every message is cryptographically verified and decentralized, so no single company controls the communication.

**Who Needs This:**
- Large AI companies (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) that run billions of conversations daily
- Decentralized networks that need AI systems to communicate securely
- Sovereign entities that want AI communication they control

**Current Status:**
- Pre-revenue (still in beta testing)
- No customers yet
- Technology is working and proven
