# Sterling Capital Intelligence (SCI)
## Complete Roadmap & Implementation Guide

## Executive Summary

Sterling Capital Intelligence (SCI) is an **AI-powered financial optimization platform** that helps high-net-worth individuals and mid-to-large corporations optimize their financial assets and achieve superior returns. As individuals and companies accumulate capital, they lack sophisticated tools to optimize portfolios, manage taxes, hedge risks, and deploy capital efficiently. SCI fills that gap by providing AI-driven analysis, recommendations, and ongoing optimization that removes financial inefficiency and generates measurable returns.

**Market Size:** 10,000+ UK high-net-worth individuals (£500K+ portfolios) + 5,000+ UK mid-market corporations (£50M+ revenue)  
**Average Deal Size:** £9,000-£13,000 per engagement (Phase 1) + £1,200-£48,000 annual subscription (Phase 2+)  
**Revenue Model:** Engagement fees + annual subscriptions + performance-based fees + white-label licensing  
**Profitability:** 70-85% gross margins (Phase 1), 65-75% gross margins (Phase 2+)

# PART 1: PHASE 1 USER GUIDE (CURRENT - £0 DEVELOPMENT, £9K-£13K PER ENGAGEMENT)

## What Sterling Capital Intelligence Phase 1 Does

Sterling Capital Intelligence Phase 1 is an **AI-assisted financial optimization service** that:

- **Analyzes** complete financial picture (all assets, all accounts, all liabilities)
- **Identifies** optimization opportunities (tax savings, fee reduction, performance improvement)
- **Calculates** financial impact (how much can be saved or gained annually)
- **Recommends** specific actions (what to do, when, how)
- **Implements** recommendations (helps execute strategy)
- **Monitors** ongoing performance (quarterly reviews and adjustments)

## How It Works - Step by Step

### For Personal Wealth Clients

#### Step 1: Discovery & Data Collection (Week 1)

Client provides complete financial picture:
- All investment accounts (stocks, bonds, funds, real estate, alternatives)
- All bank accounts and cash positions
- All liabilities (mortgages, loans, credit cards)
- Tax history (last 3 years of tax returns)
- Estate planning documents (will, trusts, insurance policies)
- Financial goals and risk tolerance

SCI team interviews client on:
- Current financial priorities and concerns
- Life stage and time horizon
- Family situation and dependents
- Existing advisors and relationships

**Deliverable:** Complete financial profile document (10-15 pages)

#### Step 2: Analysis & Opportunity Identification (Week 2)

SCI AI engine analyzes portfolio and identifies opportunities:

**Tax Optimization Opportunities:**
- Tax-loss harvesting (identify losses to offset gains)
- Tax-efficient withdrawal sequencing (order of account withdrawals)
- Charitable giving strategies (maximize tax deduction)
- Trust structure optimization (minimize estate taxes)
- Opportunity zone investments (defer capital gains)

**Fee Reduction Opportunities:**
- Current fee analysis (what you're paying across all accounts)
- Fee comparison (vs. market alternatives)
- Consolidation opportunities (reduce number of advisors)
- Fund selection optimization (lower-cost alternatives)

**Performance Improvement Opportunities:**
- Asset allocation optimization (vs. risk tolerance)
- Rebalancing recommendations (restore target allocation)
- Concentrated position reduction (diversify risk)
- Alternative investment opportunities (enhance returns)

**Risk Management Opportunities:**
- Insurance gap analysis (adequate coverage)
- Estate planning gaps (will, trusts, POA)
- Longevity risk assessment (portfolio lasting lifetime)
- Sequence of returns risk (retirement income stability)

**Deliverable:** Opportunity analysis report (20-30 pages with specific recommendations)

#### Step 3: Strategy Development (Week 3)

SCI develops personalized wealth optimization strategy:

- **Wealth Strategy Document:** Comprehensive plan addressing all opportunities
- **Tax Optimization Plan:** Specific tax-saving actions and timeline
- **Estate Planning Recommendations:** Will, trusts, insurance, beneficiary designations
- **Rebalancing Schedule:** When and what to rebalance
- **Implementation Roadmap:** Step-by-step action plan with timeline
- **Financial Projections:** Projected outcomes with and without recommendations

**Deliverable:** Comprehensive wealth strategy document (30-40 pages)

#### Step 4: Implementation Support & Ongoing Monitoring (Week 4+)

SCI helps implement recommendations:

- Coordinate with existing advisors (wealth manager, CPA, estate attorney)
- Execute trades and account changes
- Set up monitoring dashboard
- Provide quarterly check-ins
- Adjust strategy based on market changes or life changes

**Deliverable:** Implementation plan, quarterly reports, ongoing monitoring

### For Corporate Treasury Clients

#### Step 1: Discovery & Data Collection (Week 1)

CFO/Treasurer provides complete cash and debt picture:
- All bank accounts and cash positions (by currency, by entity)
- All debt instruments (amount, rate, maturity, covenants)
- FX exposure (by currency, by subsidiary, by transaction type)
- Working capital metrics (receivables, payables, inventory)
- Current banking relationships and fees
- Treasury policies and procedures
- Financial goals and constraints

SCI team interviews treasury team on:
- Current priorities and pain points
- Banking relationships and satisfaction
- Regulatory requirements and constraints
- Risk appetite and hedging philosophy
- Technology and system constraints

**Deliverable:** Complete treasury profile document (10-15 pages)

#### Step 2: Analysis & Opportunity Identification (Week 2)

SCI AI engine analyzes treasury and identifies opportunities:

**Cash Management Opportunities:**
- Bank account consolidation (reduce number of accounts)
- Cash deployment strategy (where to put excess cash)
- Money market fund opportunities (earn higher yields)
- Liquidity optimization (balance liquidity vs. returns)

**FX Risk Management Opportunities:**
- Currency exposure analysis (by currency, by amount)
- Hedging recommendations (forwards, options, natural hedges)
- Hedging cost vs. benefit analysis
- Scenario analysis (what if currency moves 10%?)

**Debt Optimization Opportunities:**
- Debt refinancing analysis (current rates vs. market rates)
- Debt maturity optimization (ladder vs. bullet)
- Covenant monitoring (alert if approaching limits)
- Debt paydown strategy (accelerate vs. maintain)

**Working Capital Optimization Opportunities:**
- Days sales outstanding (DSO) analysis and improvement
- Days inventory outstanding (DIO) analysis and improvement
- Days payable outstanding (DPO) analysis and improvement
- Cash conversion cycle optimization

**Banking & Fee Optimization Opportunities:**
- Banking fee analysis (what you're paying)
- Relationship consolidation (reduce number of banks)
- Fee negotiation opportunities
- Service level optimization

**Deliverable:** Opportunity analysis report (20-30 pages with specific recommendations)

#### Step 3: Strategy Development (Week 3)

SCI develops personalized treasury optimization strategy:

- **Treasury Strategy Document:** Comprehensive plan addressing all opportunities
- **Cash Management Plan:** Specific cash deployment and liquidity strategy
- **FX Hedging Strategy:** Specific hedging recommendations and execution plan
- **Debt Optimization Plan:** Refinancing and paydown strategy
- **Working Capital Plan:** DSO/DIO/DPO improvement targets
- **Implementation Roadmap:** Step-by-step action plan with timeline
- **Financial Projections:** Projected savings with and without recommendations

**Deliverable:** Comprehensive treasury strategy document (30-40 pages)

#### Step 4: Implementation Support & Ongoing Monitoring (Week 4+)

SCI helps implement recommendations:

- Coordinate with banks and financial advisors
- Execute cash deployments and hedges
- Refinance debt
- Set up monitoring dashboard
- Provide monthly check-ins
- Adjust strategy based on market changes or business changes

**Deliverable:** Implementation plan, monthly reports, ongoing monitoring
