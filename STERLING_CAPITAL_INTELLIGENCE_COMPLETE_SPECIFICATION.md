## Executive Summary

Sterling Capital Intelligence is a unified AI-powered financial optimization platform serving two distinct but complementary markets: personal wealth management for high-net-worth individuals and corporate treasury management for mid-market and enterprise companies.

Rather than traditional labor-intensive wealth management, SCI uses AI to analyze financial assets, identify optimization opportunities, and provide recurring insights through a subscription-based software model.

**Key Differentiator:** One platform, two markets, software-scalable revenue model.

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

#### 1. Personal Wealth Intelligence (B2C)
- **Target:** High-net-worth individuals (£500K-£10M+ net worth)
- **Subscription:** £500-£2,000/month
- **One-time setup:** £5,000-£10,000 for comprehensive wealth analysis
- **Performance fees:** 1-2% of identified optimization gains (optional)

#### 2. Corporate Treasury Intelligence (B2B)
- **Target:** Mid-market and enterprise companies (£10M-£1B+ revenue)
- **Subscription:** £2,000-£10,000/month depending on company size
- **One-time setup:** £10,000-£25,000 for comprehensive treasury analysis
- **Performance fees:** 0.5-1% of identified cash optimization gains (optional)

#### 3. Family Office & Institutional (B2B2C)
- **Target:** Family offices, pension funds, investment advisors
- **Subscription:** £5,000-£20,000/month
- **White-label options available**
- **Performance fees:** Tiered based on AUM

## User Experience Flow

### Personal Wealth Intelligence

**Onboarding (Week 1):**
1. Client creates account and completes financial profile questionnaire
2. Client connects bank and investment accounts (or uploads statements)
3. AI performs initial comprehensive analysis
4. Client receives welcome report with top 10 recommendations
5. Client schedules optional consultation call

**Ongoing (Monthly):**
1. AI continuously monitors portfolio and market conditions
2. New recommendations appear in dashboard feed
3. Client reviews recommendations and implements as desired
4. Monthly performance report generated
5. Quarterly deep-dive analysis available

**Engagement:**
- Dashboard login: 2-3x per week
- Email notifications: Weekly digest of top recommendations
- Quarterly review calls: Optional, with SCI advisor
- Annual comprehensive review: Included with subscription

### Corporate Treasury Intelligence

**Onboarding (Weeks 1-2):**
1. CFO/Treasurer creates account and completes company profile
2. Company connects treasury systems (SAP, Oracle, etc.) or uploads data
3. AI performs initial comprehensive cash and debt analysis
4. Company receives welcome report with top 15 recommendations
5. Company schedules implementation kickoff call

**Ongoing (Weekly/Monthly):**
1. AI continuously monitors cash positions and market conditions
2. New recommendations appear in dashboard feed
3. Treasury team reviews and implements recommendations
4. Weekly cash flow forecast updated
5. Monthly performance report generated
6. Quarterly strategic review available

**Engagement:**
- Dashboard login: Daily
- Email alerts: Real-time for critical items, daily digest
- Weekly check-in calls: With SCI treasury specialist
- Monthly strategy meetings: With CFO/Treasurer
- Quarterly board reporting: Optimization impact summary

## Competitive Advantages

### vs. Traditional Wealth Managers
- **Cost:** 0.5-1% subscription vs. 1-2% AUM
- **Accessibility:** Serves £500K+ vs. £1M+ minimum
- **Transparency:** Clear recommendations with reasoning vs. black-box advice
- **Speed:** Real-time analysis vs. quarterly reviews
- **Scalability:** Software-based vs. advisor-dependent

### vs. Robo-Advisors
- **Sophistication:** Advanced tax optimization, estate planning, treasury analysis
- **Personalization:** Customized to individual/corporate situation
- **Hybrid:** Combines AI with optional human advisors
- **Breadth:** Covers wealth, treasury, tax, estate planning
- **Institutional:** Serves both individuals and corporations

### vs. Corporate Treasury Software
- **AI-Powered:** Recommendations vs. just data aggregation
- **Holistic:** Covers cash, debt, FX, investments, compliance
- **Ease of Use:** Intuitive dashboard vs. complex enterprise software
- **Cost:** Subscription vs. enterprise licensing
- **Implementation:** Quick deployment vs. 6-12 month implementations
