## Executive Summary

Sterling Global Intelligence is a **real-time supply chain tracking platform** that helps you:
- Track shipments and vessels globally
- Monitor port operations
- Assess geopolitical risks
- Predict disruptions
- Score shipment risks for insurance

The platform has **three implementation phases**, each adding more capabilities and data sources. This guide explains all three phases so you understand the current state and future potential.
