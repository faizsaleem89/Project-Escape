# PART 1: PHASE 1 USER GUIDE (Current - $0/month)

## What Is Sterling Estate Intelligence?

Sterling Estate Intelligence is a **comprehensive land development intelligence platform**. Think of it like a database where you can search and analyze:
- Available development land across the UK
- Property development potential and profitability
- Planning constraints and risks
- Competitive opportunities
- Feasibility and investment analysis

---

## The 4 Main Pages Explained

### 1. **Dashboard** (Overview & Analytics)
**What you see:** Key metrics and insights at a glance
- Total available properties
- Development opportunities by region
- Average development potential
- Risk distribution
- Market trends
- Recent opportunities

**How to use it:** Start here to understand the current market landscape and identify hot zones.

---

### 2. **Search** (Find Development Opportunities)
**What you see:** Search and filter available development land

You can search by:
- Location (city, postcode, region)
- Land size (0.5-1 acre, 1-2 acres, 2-5 acres, 5+ acres)
- Budget (£100K, £250K, £500K, £1M+)
- Development type (Residential, Commercial, Mixed-use, Industrial)
- Planning status (Available, Pending, Approved)

**Result:** You'll get a list of properties matching your criteria with:
- Location and size
- Current owner/status
- Estimated development potential
- Risk level
- Quick analysis

**How to use it:** Use this to find specific opportunities that match your investment criteria.

---

### 3. **Property Detail** (In-Depth Analysis)
**What you see:** Comprehensive analysis of a specific property

For each property, you see:
- **Location details** (address, postcode, coordinates)
- **Land information** (size, shape, current use, ownership)
- **Development potential** (estimated units, GDV, build costs, profit)
- **Planning constraints** (flood zones, conservation areas, listed buildings)
- **Accessibility** (roads, transport, utilities)
- **Market analysis** (comparable properties, local demand)
- **Risk assessment** (planning risk, environmental risk, market risk)
- **Feasibility score** (0-100, higher = more viable)

**How to use it:** Click on any property from the search results to see detailed analysis before making investment decisions.

---

### 4. **Reports** (Generate Feasibility Reports)
**What you see:** Generate and download comprehensive feasibility reports

You can generate reports for:
- Individual properties
- Multiple properties (comparison)
- Market analysis by region
- Investment portfolio analysis

**Report includes:**
- Executive summary
- Property details and analysis
- Development potential calculations
- Risk assessment
- Financial projections
- Recommendations
- PDF export for sharing with investors/partners

**How to use it:** Generate reports for due diligence, investor presentations, or internal analysis.

---

## Quick Start Guide - Phase 1

### Scenario 1: "I want to find development land in Manchester"
1. Go to **Search** page
2. Enter location: "Manchester"
3. Set land size: "1-2 acres"
4. Set budget: "£500K"
5. Set type: "Residential"
6. Click "Search"
7. See results with development potential

### Scenario 2: "Is this property worth developing?"
1. Go to **Search** and find the property
2. Click on property to see **Property Detail**
3. Check feasibility score
4. Review development potential (units, GDV, profit)
5. Check risk assessment
6. Read recommendations

### Scenario 3: "I need to present this to investors"
1. Go to **Reports** page
2. Select property
3. Choose "Feasibility Report"
4. Click "Generate Report"
5. Download PDF
6. Share with investors

### Scenario 4: "What's the best opportunity right now?"
1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. See top opportunities by region
3. Check market trends
4. Identify hot zones
5. Go to **Search** to explore further
