### Step 2: Choose Your Registration Method

You have two options:

#### **Option A: Online Registration (Recommended)**

**Website:** https://beta.companieshouse.gov.uk/

**Time:** 15-30 minutes  
**Cost:** £12 (online fee)  
**Processing time:** 24-48 hours

**Steps:**
1. Go to Companies House website
2. Click "Register a company"
3. Choose "Limited company"
4. Enter company details
5. Enter shareholder information
6. Enter director information
7. Choose registered office address
8. Select SIC codes
9. Pay £12 online
10. Receive confirmation

#### **Option B: Paper Registration**

**Form:** Form IN01  
**Cost:** £40 (paper fee)  
**Processing time:** 5-10 working days

**Not recommended** — slower and more expensive than online.

**Recommendation:** Use Option A (Online Registration) — faster, cheaper, easier.

#### **4. Create Internal Documents**

- Shareholder agreement (between you and Umar Latif)

### Ongoing Obligations (Annual)

#### **1. Annual Accounts**

- File accounts with Companies House every year
- Deadline: 9 months after year-end
- Cost: Free to file online

#### **2. Annual Return**

- File confirmation statement with Companies House every year
- Deadline: 14 days after anniversary of incorporation
- Cost: Free to file online

#### **3. Tax Return**

- File Corporation Tax return with HMRC
- Deadline: 12 months after year-end
- Pay any tax owed

#### **4. Payroll (If You Have Employees)**

- Register as an employer with HMRC
- Run payroll monthly
- File payroll information quarterly

## PART 5: DIVISION STRUCTURE AND OPERATIONS

### How the Seven Divisions Work

Each division operates as a **business unit** within Sterling Intelligence Group Limited. They are NOT separate companies.

#### **Division Operations**

| Division | What It Does | How It Works | Revenue Model |
|----------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Lexis AI** | Legal contract analysis | Clients upload contracts, AI analyzes for risks | Per-contract fee (£500-£2,000) or retainer |
| **Cyber Governance** | AI security audits | Companies provide info, system audits for shadow AI | Initial audit (£5k-£15k) + ongoing monitoring |
| **Revenue Recovery** | Billing optimization | Firms enter data, system identifies unbilled time | Audit fee + performance-based fee |
| **Estate Intelligence** | Property analysis | Users enter property details, system analyzes investment potential | Per-property fee (£1k-£2k) |
| **Global Intelligence** | Supply chain risk | Users enter business info, system assesses supply chain risks | Audit fee + strategy fee |
| **Private Office** | Wealth management | High-net-worth clients, portfolio management | 0.5% of assets under management |
| **Trade Growth Studio** | Export strategy | Businesses enter details, system develops export strategy | Strategy fee + implementation fee |
| **Brand Audit Service** | Brand analysis | Companies answer questionnaire, AI generates audit report | Per-report fee (£2k-£5k) |

#### **How Revenue Flows**

1. **Client pays Sterling Intelligence Group Limited** (the parent company)
2. **Money goes into company bank account**
3. **Expenses for each division are tracked separately**
4. **Profits are calculated for each division**
5. **At year-end, total company profit is calculated**
6. **Dividends are paid to shareholders (50% to you, 50% to Umar Latif)**
