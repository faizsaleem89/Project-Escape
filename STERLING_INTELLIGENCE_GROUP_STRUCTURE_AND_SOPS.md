## PART 3: STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES (SOPs)

### SOP 1: LEXIS AI — CONTRACT ANALYSIS PROCESS

**Purpose:** Standardize how contract analysis is performed

**Process Steps:**

1. **Client Onboarding**
   - Client submits contract (PDF or Word document)
   - Client provides context (industry, contract type, concerns)
   - Client pays upfront (£500-£2,000 depending on contract length)

2. **Contract Upload & Preparation**
   - Contract is uploaded to secure system
   - Document is scanned for completeness
   - Any missing pages are requested

3. **AI Analysis**
   - AI system analyzes contract for:
     - Legal risks (liability, indemnification)
     - Compliance gaps (regulatory requirements)
     - Financial risks (payment terms, penalties)
     - Termination clauses and conditions
     - Intellectual property issues
     - Confidentiality provisions

4. **Risk Matrix Generation**
   - System generates visual Risk Matrix showing:
     - High-risk clauses (red)
     - Medium-risk clauses (yellow)
     - Low-risk clauses (green)
   - Each risk is scored 1-10

5. **Recommendations**
   - System provides specific recommendations:
     - Clauses to negotiate
     - Clauses to remove
     - Clauses to add
     - Legal review needed (yes/no)

6. **Report Delivery**
   - Report is delivered within 24 hours
   - Format: PDF with visual Risk Matrix
   - Includes executive summary and detailed analysis

7. **Follow-Up**
   - Client receives report
   - Client can request clarification (included in service)
   - Optional: Legal consultation with partner law firm (additional fee)

**Quality Assurance:**
- Every report is reviewed by a legal expert
- Risk scores are verified for accuracy
- Recommendations are checked for completeness

**Timeline:** 24 hours from submission to delivery

**Purpose:** Standardize AI security and compliance audits

**Process Steps:**

1. **Initial Consultation**
   - Company provides: name, industry, employee count, current AI usage
   - Consultant explains audit scope and timeline
   - Client pays initial audit fee (£5,000-£15,000)

2. **Data Collection**
   - Company completes detailed questionnaire:
     - What AI tools are currently authorized?
     - What AI tools are employees using without approval?
     - What data is being shared with AI systems?
     - What are current compliance requirements?
     - What is current insurance coverage?

3. **Risk Assessment**
   - System analyzes responses and identifies:
     - Shadow AI usage (unauthorized tools)
     - Data exposure risks
     - Compliance gaps (GDPR, industry regulations)
     - Insurance coverage gaps
     - Governance framework gaps

4. **Audit Report Generation**
   - Report includes:
     - Executive summary
     - Risk assessment (high/medium/low)
     - Compliance gaps identified
     - Recommended governance framework
     - Implementation timeline

5. **Recommendations Presentation**
   - Consultant presents findings to company leadership
   - Discusses recommended governance policies
   - Answers questions and concerns

6. **Policy Development** (Additional Service)
   - If company wants to proceed, Sterling develops:
     - AI usage policy
     - Data handling procedures
     - Approval process for new AI tools
     - Employee training materials
   - Cost: £10,000-£25,000

7. **Ongoing Monitoring** (Optional)
   - Monthly compliance checks
   - Quarterly policy reviews
   - Annual audit refresh
   - Cost: £750-£2,500/month

**Quality Assurance:**
- Every audit is reviewed by compliance expert
- Risk assessments are verified
- Recommendations are tested against industry standards

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to final report

### SOP 3: REVENUE RECOVERY — BILLING AUDIT PROCESS

**Purpose:** Standardize billing optimization for professional service firms

**Process Steps:**

1. **Client Onboarding**

   - Firm provides:
     - Time tracking data (last 12 months)
     - Billing data (invoices, rates, payment records)
     - Matter allocation data
     - Client list with billing rates

3. **Analysis**
   - System analyzes:
     - Unbilled hours (typically 15% of billable time)
     - Underutilized billing codes
     - Client matter misallocations
     - Administrative time gaps
     - Billing rate optimization opportunities

4. **Opportunity Identification**
   - System calculates:
     - Total recovery potential (typically 5-15% of annual revenue)
     - Quick wins (easy to implement)
     - Long-term improvements
     - ROI for each recommendation

5. **Report Generation**
   - Report includes:
     - Executive summary with recovery potential
     - Detailed analysis of findings
     - Specific recommendations
     - Implementation roadmap
     - Expected ROI

6. **Implementation** (Additional Service)
   - If firm wants to proceed, Sterling helps:
     - Set up new billing codes
     - Implement time tracking improvements
     - Train staff on billing procedures
     - Monitor results
   - Cost: £5,000-£10,000

7. **Performance Tracking**
   - Monthly reports showing:
     - Recovered revenue
     - Progress vs. targets
     - Recommendations for further improvement
   - Performance-based fee: 10-15% of recovered revenue

**Quality Assurance:**
- Every audit is reviewed by accounting expert
- Calculations are verified
- Recommendations are tested for feasibility

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks from data collection to final report
