## Part 2: Divisions 4-7

### Division 4: Sterling Global Intelligence
**Target:** UK manufacturers and retailers
**Problem:** Global supply chains constantly at risk (Brexit, geopolitical tensions, climate change, pandemics). A single disruption can halt production for weeks/months. Cost of disruption can exceed £5 million for mid-sized firms.
**Solution:** Map entire supply chain, identify vulnerabilities, identify alternative suppliers globally, build "Plan B" for resilience
**Results:** Resilient supply chain; if primary supplier goes down, switch to backup within hours; production never stops
**Fee:** £18,000 setup + £2,000/month ongoing monitoring
**Real Example:** Tech manufacturer in Midlands - identified primary supplier in geopolitically unstable region, vetted alternatives in Mexico and Poland. 6 months later, original supplier hit by strike. Switched to backup, lost zero production time, saved ~£3 million.

### Division 5: Sterling Estate Intelligence
**Target:** UK property developers and investors
**Problem:** Finding best development sites requires sifting through thousands of planning applications and land registry records - process takes weeks/months for human teams. Developers who find sites first win.
**Solution:** Use AI to scan all planning portals and land registries across target regions, identify sites with high development potential, cross-reference with market data, deliver ranked list of opportunities with feasibility blueprints
**Results:** Curated list of best development sites delivered weekly; find opportunities competitors miss; move faster, negotiate better prices, secure most profitable deals
**Fee:** £15,000 initial scan + £2,000/month for ongoing monitoring and weekly reports
**Real Example:** Developer in North West - identified 0.8-acre brownfield site in Stockport that had been overlooked for years. Acquired for £1.2M, secured planning permission for 45 units, projected GDV £12M. Single site discovery paid for 3 years of service.

## Key Insight About Sterling Estate Intelligence & Worldwide Scalability

**What I built for Sterling Estate Intelligence:**
- Scans UK planning portals and land registries
- Identifies high-potential development sites
- Cross-references with market data
- Delivers ranked opportunity reports

**Can it work worldwide?**
YES. The core concept is universal:
- Every country has planning/land registry systems
- Every country has property market data
- The AI scanning and analysis process is the same
- You just adapt the data sources to each country's systems

**Expansion Strategy:**
- Start with UK (proven model)
- Expand to other English-speaking markets (Australia, Canada, US)
- Then expand to European markets
- Each region would have its own data sources but same core AI logic

This is actually a massive opportunity for scaling Sterling Intelligence Group globally.
