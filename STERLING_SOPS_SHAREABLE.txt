## DIVISION 1: LEXIS AI

**Business:** Legal contract analysis and risk identification

**What We Deliver:**
Clients upload legal contracts and receive a comprehensive Risk Matrix report identifying legal risks, compliance gaps, and liability issues. The report includes specific recommendations for contract negotiation or revision.

**Service Process:**
1. Client submits contract and provides context
2. Payment is collected upfront (£500-£2,000 depending on contract length)
3. AI system analyzes contract for legal risks and compliance gaps
4. Risk Matrix report is generated showing high/medium/low risk areas
5. Specific recommendations are provided
6. Report is delivered within 24 hours
7. Client can request clarification or follow-up analysis

**Revenue:**
- Per-contract analysis: £500-£2,000
- Monthly retainer: £2,000-£5,000
- Enterprise licensing: Custom pricing

**Target Customers:** Law firms, in-house legal teams, contract-heavy businesses

**Quality Assurance:** Every report is reviewed by a legal expert before delivery

## DIVISION 2: CYBER GOVERNANCE

**Business:** AI security audits and governance framework development

**What We Deliver:**
Companies receive a comprehensive audit identifying unauthorized AI usage ("shadow AI"), compliance gaps, and governance risks. We then develop customized AI usage policies and compliance frameworks.

**Service Process:**
1. Initial consultation with company leadership
2. Company completes detailed questionnaire about current AI usage
3. System analyzes responses and identifies shadow AI and compliance risks
4. Comprehensive audit report is generated
5. Consultant presents findings to company leadership
6. If approved, Sterling develops AI governance policies
7. Optional ongoing monitoring service tracks compliance

**Revenue:**
- Initial audit: £5,000-£15,000
- Policy development: £10,000-£25,000
- Ongoing monitoring: £750-£2,500/month
- Enterprise licensing: Custom pricing

**Target Customers:** Financial services, legal firms, healthcare, regulated industries

**Quality Assurance:** Every audit is reviewed by compliance expert

---

## DIVISION 3: REVENUE RECOVERY

**Business:** Billing optimization for professional service firms

**What We Deliver:**
Professional firms (law, accounting, consulting) receive a detailed audit identifying unbilled hours and lost revenue opportunities. We calculate recovery potential and provide implementation support.

**Service Process:**
1. Firm provides time tracking and billing data from last 12 months
2. Payment is collected (£3,000-£5,000 audit fee)
3. System analyzes billing patterns and identifies unbilled hours
4. Recovery opportunities are calculated (typically 5-15% of annual revenue)
5. Comprehensive audit report is generated with recommendations
6. Optional: Sterling helps implement improvements and tracks results
7. Performance-based fees can apply to recovered revenue

**Revenue:**
- Billing audit: £3,000-£5,000
- Implementation & setup: £5,000-£10,000
- Ongoing optimization: £500-£1,000/month
- Performance-based fee: 10-15% of recovered revenue

**Target Customers:** Law firms, accounting firms, consulting practices

**Quality Assurance:** Every audit is reviewed by accounting expert
